Welcome to the Toy Robot Game!
==============================
Get started with these instructions:
- The PLACE command will put the toy robot on the table in position X,Y and facing NORTH, SOUTH, EAST or WEST.
- The first valid command to the robot is a PLACE command, after that, any sequence of commands
  may be issued, in any order, including another PLACE command.
- MOVE will move the toy robot one unit forward in the direction it is currently facing.
- LEFT and RIGHT will rotate the robot 90 degrees in the specified direction without changing the position of the robot.
- REPORT will announce the X,Y and orientation of the robot.
- EXIT will exit the application.
